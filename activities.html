Activities for you!


Meet the Panther


Love panthers? Yes? It's your lucky day! Meet our black panther "Kenji", here at the Dehiwala Zoo.


Kenji loves his visitors, so make sure to come and say hi to him!!!


PRICE: 2000LKR per adult(ages 18+).

PRICE: 1500LKR per child(ages 17 and below).

TIME: 4.00-5.00pm, daily.

Feed the elephants


We believe everyone loves our elephant friends. Come say hi and feed them!

Food allowed:

  • Fruits
  • Milk
  • Tree barks

PRICE: 1500LKR per adult(ages 18+).

PRICE: 1400LKR per child(ages 17 and below).

TIME: 10.00am-12.00pm, daily.


Birthday parties at the zoo!


Take your birthday party out of hotels or homes and join with the nature. Children can enjoy the beautiful environment of Dehiwala Zoological Gardens.
Two Birthday Party Packages are available:

  • Lawn with Animals- LKR 5,000.
  • Animals - Guinea Pigs, Rabbits, Tortoise, Brahmini kites, Cockatoos, Python etc.
  • Lawn without Animals- LKR 3,000.
  • You can bring your own refreshments or can receive from our food outlets.
  • Select your need and contact Assistant Director (Dehiwala Zoo).

Send your request through zoosl@slt.lk, by hand, post or fax.
